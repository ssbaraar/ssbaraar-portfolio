## 1. Core Positioning

You should be positioned as:

> A technical GTM and GenAI engineer who builds lead intelligence, AI automation, RAG, agentic workflows, and CRM automation for companies that have business pain but not enough internal engineering capacity.

Short version:

> I build the AI and automation layer between messy business workflows and revenue outcomes.

What this means in plain business language:

- For revenue teams: you automate prospecting, enrichment, scoring, routing, and follow-up.
- For operations teams: you automate document intake, data entry, internal search, and repetitive workflows.
- For AI/product teams: you build GenAI features, RAG systems, agents, and FastAPI integrations.

## 2. Your Best ICP Split

You need two ICPs:

1. Recruiter/job ICP: companies hiring remote GenAI/GTM/Applied AI engineers.
